Passionate about and Thriving with Analytic Apps

What are they?

Analytic apps, also known as analytical applications and data apps, provide businesses with pre-packaged tools like self-service dashboards and data visualizations to enhance operational performance. These tools aid regular business users in any industries by offering data-driven insights for informed decision-making. They're adaptable across various industries and systems, filling workflow gaps and aiding action with deep domain knowledge. Whether created in-house or obtained from a third-party, analytical applications serve as specialized analytics platforms for businesses.

View Example

How do they work?

Analytical apps work by providing accessible, ready-to-use business intelligence (BI) experiences tailored for diverse users, featuring a user-friendly design with consistent layouts focused on key metrics. These apps facilitate self-service exploration for uncovering insights, often incorporating intuitive wizards to guide users through analysis. For instance, a hiring manager might utilize a custom analytical app displaying data on interactions with recruitment firms, including recruitment numbers, locations, and retention rates. This targeted app ensures users gain maximum value from available data for informed decision-making, rather than overwhelming them with numerous reports and dashboards. In simple terms, it empowers users to experience with reportings in one place.

Where do they fit well?

Analytical applications find their place in diverse domains where data-driven insights are crucial. These versatile tools are integral to business intelligence, aiding financial analysis, marketing optimization, operations management, customer relationship management, human resources, healthcare analytics, retail analytics, supply chain management, and others. Their adaptability spans across industries and functions, empowering organizations to extract meaningful insights and make informed decisions.

why do we need them?

Customized analytic apps offer unique experiences because regular IT-led BI solutions may not fit well with today's average business user. These apps focus on providing analytics tailored to individual needs, ensuring more value from data, and can even be branded with your businesses. By bundling important BI features into an easy-to-use software package, they make it easier for businesses to adopt BI, speed up getting insights for making decisions, and quickly deliver effiecient value to customers. Choosing the right app with all the required functionality boosts business operations and provides cost effective solutions to the businesses.

Long Story Short

Analytical applications play a pivotal role in enhancing operational performance for businesses across diverse industries. They offer user-friendly, ready-to-use business intelligence experiences, empowering regular users to gain valuable insights and data-driven results to support informed decision-making. Their adaptability makes them suitable and versatile for various domains, including financial analysis, marketing optimization, operations management, and much more. The need for customized analytic apps arises from the limitations of traditional IT-led BI solutions, as these apps focus on tailoring analytics to individual needs, ensuring optimal value from data, and providing efficient solutions. Choosing the right app with the necessary functionality not only facilitates BI experience but also accelerates insights delivery, contributing to effective and cost-efficient business operations.
