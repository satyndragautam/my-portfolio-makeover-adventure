Youth Tobacco Use in USA
After finishing my Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate, I wanted to brush up my skills in building reports using Tableau. So, my goal was to utilize public dataset and explore it and represent valuable insights through a simple dashboard in Tableau. The YTS was developed to provide states with comprehensive data on both middle school and high school students regarding tobacco use, exposure to environmental tobacco smoke, smoking cessation, school curriculum, minors' ability to purchase or otherwise obtain tobacco products, knowledge, and attitudes about tobacco, and familiarity with pro-tobacco and anti-tobacco media messages. The YTS uses a two-stage cluster sample design to produce representative samples of students in middle schools (grades 6 to 8) and high schools (grades 9 to 12). Tobacco topics included are cigarette smoking prevalence, cigarette smoking frequency, smokeless tobacco products prevalence, and quit attempts.
